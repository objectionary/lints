Atom without rt meta

Defined atoms must have a +rt meta.

Incorrect:

+architect jeff@foo.com
+home https://github.com/objectionary/eo
+package org.eolang
+version 0.0.0

[] > bytes
  [x] > eq ?

  [y] > not ?

Correct:

+architect jeff@foo.com
+home https://github.com/objectionary/eo
+package org.eolang
+version 0.0.0
+rt jvm org.eolang:eo-runtime:0.43.2

[] > bytes
  [x] > eq ?

  [y] > not ?
