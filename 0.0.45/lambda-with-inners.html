Lambda With Inners

In XMIR, atom object cannot have inner objects inside lambda object.

Incorrect:

<program>
  <objects>
    <o name="obj">
      <o name="λ">
        <o name="foo"/>
      </o>
    </o>
  </objects>
</program>

Correct:

<program>
  <objects>
    <o name="obj">
      <o name="λ"/>
    </o>
  </objects>
</program>
