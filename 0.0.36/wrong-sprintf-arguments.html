Wrong QQ.txt.sprintf Arguments

The QQ.txt.sprintf object must have a match between its placeholder variables and passed arguments.

Incorrect:

[] > app
  QQ.io.stdout > @
    QQ.txt.sprintf
      "Hello, %s! Your account is %d."
      * name

Correct:

[] > app
  QQ.io.stdout > @
    QQ.txt.sprintf
      "Hello, %s! Your account is %d."
      * name acc
